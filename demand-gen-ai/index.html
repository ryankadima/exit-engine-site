AGENT 3

Lead Scoring & Routing

Traditional lead scoring is broken. Marketing scores on activity. Sales scores on fit. Nobody agrees, and MQLs pile up that never convert.

This agent replaces the whole mess with a single composite score (1-100) that weighs both signal history and ICP fit ‚Äî and writes a human-readable note for every lead.

For custom implementation book a call.

How The Scoring Works

Clay enriches every lead with firmographic and technographic data ‚Äî company size, industry, tech stack, role, seniority.

Claude reads the full signal history from Agent 2 and assigns a composite score based on four dimensions:

  • Recency ‚Äî When did they last engage? (Today vs. 3 weeks ago)
  • Frequency ‚Äî How many touchpoints? (Visited pricing page once vs. opened 4 emails + visited 3 pages)
  • Channel Diversity ‚Äî Are they showing up in multiple places? (LinkedIn + email + website = high intent)
  • ICP Fit ‚Äî Do they match your ideal customer profile? (Right company size, right role, right industry)
ü§ñ Claude Prompt ‚Äî Lead Scoring You are a demand gen analyst. Here is a lead's profile and signal history: Profile: [Clay enrichment data ‚Äî company, role, industry, size, tech stack] Signal history: [list of touchpoints with timestamps ‚Äî page visits, email opens/clicks, LinkedIn engagement, ad clicks, content downloads] Score this lead 1-100 using this framework:
‚Äî Recency (0-25): Last engagement within 24hrs = 25, within 7 days = 15, within 30 days = 5, older = 0
‚Äî Frequency (0-25): 5+ touchpoints = 25, 3-4 = 15, 1-2 = 5
‚Äî Channel Diversity (0-25): 3+ channels = 25, 2 channels = 15, 1 channel = 5
‚Äî ICP Fit (0-25): Perfect match = 25, partial = 15, weak = 5 Output: Score (number), one-paragraph CRM note explaining the score, and recommended next action. Example output: "VP Marketing at $40M SaaS company. Visited pricing page 3x this week, opened last 4 emails, engaged with LinkedIn post on ROI frameworks. Score: 87. Recommend: immediate AE outreach ‚Äî this account is in active evaluation."

The key insight: This isn't just a number. Claude writes a CRM note in plain English so your AE knows exactly why this lead is hot, what content they engaged with, and what to say on the first call. No more guessing. No more "so what do you do?"
